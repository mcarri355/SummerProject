I went camping with some of my closest family and friends in Sedona, Arizona. We camped at a place called Cave Springs Campground in Coconino National Forest. We have come camping here every year in june for around the last 20 years. (I had a rough couple of days)

At The Campground there is a creek that is absolutely freezing. There is also a trail that will take you to a cliff jumping area in which you will regret and not regret jumping off of at the same time.
